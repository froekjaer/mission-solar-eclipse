# Invitation to Independent Examination

Mission Framework Foundation v1.0 is being submitted for independent examination.

Mistral AI is invited to act as an Independent Examiner. The purpose is not to validate or endorse the framework, but to challenge its scientific basis, engineering coherence, novelty claims, assumptions, weaknesses, failure modes and evidence requirements.

The examination should distinguish observations, interpretations, recommendations and open questions. Participation must not be interpreted as endorsement of Mission Framework, its contributors or its conclusions.

We are not asking the examiner to agree. We are asking the examiner to help us become less wrong.
